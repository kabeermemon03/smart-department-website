Academic Programs

Comprehensive education pathways in electronics engineering

Bachelor of Engineering

4 Years

Comprehensive undergraduate program covering fundamentals of electronics, circuits, and systems design.

  • Circuit Analysis & Design
  • Digital Electronics
  • Microprocessors & Embedded Systems
  • Communication Systems

Master of Engineering

2 Years

Advanced graduate program focusing on specialized areas of electronics engineering and research.

  • Advanced Signal Processing
  • VLSI Design
  • Power Electronics
  • Research Methodology

Doctor of Philosophy

3-5 Years

Research-intensive doctoral program for advanced study and original research in electronics engineering.

  • Independent Research
  • Advanced Coursework
  • Dissertation Writing
  • Academic Publications

Program Learning Outcomes

"Excellence in education through measurable learning achievements"

01

Technical Knowledge

Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering fundamentals to solve complex electronics engineering problems.

02

Problem Analysis

Identify, formulate, and analyze complex engineering problems using principles of electronics and related sciences.

03

Design Solutions

Design solutions for complex engineering problems considering public health, safety, and environmental factors.

04

Research & Investigation

Conduct investigations of complex problems using research-based knowledge and methods including design of experiments.

05

Modern Tools

Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering tools for complex activities.

06

Professional Ethics

Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics, responsibilities, and norms of engineering practice.
